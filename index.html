About La Piazza

A Family Tradition
Born in Naples

Since 1987, La Piazza has been bringing the heart of Naples to every plate. Our master pizzaiolos hand-stretch each dough ball, layering flavors that have been perfected across generations.

We source only the finest ingredients: '00' flour milled in Italy, DOP-certified mozzarella di bufala, and hand-crushed San Marzano tomatoes. Every pizza tells a story — and we want yours to be unforgettable.
